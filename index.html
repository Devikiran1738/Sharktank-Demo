🦈 Shark Tank School Event – Guide & Key Terms

✅ Basic Structure of the Event

Phase

What Happens

Ideation

Students come up with product or business ideas to solve real-world problems, and make a ppt on it.

Prototype Creation

Students build a working model (physical or digital) of their idea.

Pitch Preparation

They prepare a 3–5 minute business pitch.

Pitch Day

Students present their idea to a panel of "Sharks" (judges) who ask questions and give feedback.

Judging & Investment (Points or Tokens)

Sharks invest virtual money or tokens based on idea quality, feasibility, and presentation.
